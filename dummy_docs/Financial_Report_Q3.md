# KnowledgeForge | Financial Summary Q3 2025

## 1. Executive Summary
Revenue grew 45% YoY to $12.4M. This was driven primarily by Enterprise adoption of the new 'Copilot Dashboard' (Next.js components) and API-only licenses for the 'Data Connector Suite'.

## 2. Revenue Breakdown
- **Enterprise Subscriptions:** $8.2M (66% of total)
- **API Pay-Go:** $2.4M (19% of total)
- **Professional Services:** $1.8M (15% of total)

## 3. Expense Analysis (R&D focus)
- **AI Infrastructure (OpenAI, Anthropic):** $3.8M (up 80% since Q2 due to Claude-3-Haiku usage spike).
- **Backend Scaling (PostgreSQL, Supabase):** $1.2M.
- **Human Resources (Data Science Team):** $2.4M.

## 4. Projections Q4
Expect to reach 5,000 active 'Forger' users by year-end. Primary expansion will follow the 'Figma Sync' feature launch.

## 5. Risk Assessment
Volatility in AI token costs remains the primary risk for the 'Copilot' business model.
